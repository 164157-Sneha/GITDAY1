Receipe for Veg Pulav

  • Vegetables
  • Oil
  • Masala items
  • salt
  • water
  • green chilles
  • rice

Steps to prepare

  1. Place a cooker on the stove
  2. pour 2-3 spoons of oil and put mustard
  3. grind all the masala items and green chiilies in a mixer jar put the masala items along with salt into the cooker and pour required amount of water
  4. at last put the rice into the cooker and boil it well
  5. ur Favourite pulav will be ready to eat
